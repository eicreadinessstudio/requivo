The problem

Most organisations treat documentation and knowledge as the same thing. They are not. Document management tools capture what people choose to write down. They do not capture the judgment, exceptions, and workarounds that experts carry in practice.

Expertise that disappears

When a long-tenured expert leaves, the organisation loses the judgment, workarounds, and edge-case decisions they built over years of practice. In most companies, that knowledge was never written down — and is rarely captured before it disappears.

Irreplaceable · Permanent · Unrecoverable

Knowledge that never transfers

New team members inherit the manual, but not the judgment behind it. The gap between documented procedure and how work actually gets done is rarely captured — which forces every new hire to relearn it from scratch.

Lost productivity · Infinite restart

Answers that exist but can't be found

In organisations, the answer to many operational questions already exists — in someone’s head, two floors away or two time zones apart. Without a way to route questions to the right expertise, that knowledge remains inaccessible.

Operational risk · Repeated cost

Product

An operational knowledge system for governed operational knowledge construction.
01 — Construct

Governed operational knowledge construction

Requivo performs governed operational knowledge construction from fragmented documents, workflow evidence, and expert input. It helps structure, validate, and preserve knowledge in a form that can be reused across the organisation.

02 — Route

Expert routing and reuse

When a question arises, Requivo uses the governed operational knowledge layer first. If the answer is still unresolved, it routes the issue to the relevant expertise and returns the validated result into the layer.
