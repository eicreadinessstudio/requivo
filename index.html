Solution
One governed knowledge layer, built from the knowledge you already have

Requivo works where your critical knowledge already lives — across documents, systems, workflows, and people.

It structures fragmented evidence, detects gaps and conflicts, and routes unresolved areas to the right expert for confirmation.

The result is not a one-off answer. It is a reusable knowledge layer your teams can use for answers, procedures, onboarding, handovers, and controlled AI access.

Core flow

Fragmented evidence → governed knowledge

Connect what's scattered and turn it into maintained, validated operational knowledge.

Validation loop

Gaps become expert tasks

Weak, missing, or conflicting knowledge is surfaced and routed to the named expert who should confirm it.

Application layer

One layer, many uses

The same governed layer powers verified answers, living procedures, expert review tasks, onboarding, handover, and controlled AI access.

Governance
Designed for governance from day one
Requivo is built around the controls complex organisations need before knowledge can be reused by teams, workflows, and AI systems.
01

Provenance

Every answer remains linked to its source material, so teams can see where knowledge came from and why it is trusted.

02

Validation state

Knowledge can be draft, verified, under review, or retired — instead of being treated as equally reliable by default.

03

Expert ownership

Open questions are routed to accountable experts, turning missing or conflicting knowledge into reviewable decisions.

04

Audit history

Decisions, confirmations, updates, and reuse signals remain traceable over time.

Why Requivo
Why better search is not enough
Search tools find documents. AI assistants generate answers. Documentation tools store what people write. Requivo starts earlier: it makes organisational knowledge trustworthy before people and AI reuse it.
Fragmented
Critical knowledge is distributed across files, systems, workflows, and experts — often without a single maintained structure.
Weakly structured
Existing information rarely reflects real operational hierarchy, ownership, currentness, and reliability.
Expert-dependent
Operational judgment, exceptions, and edge-case reasoning often remain with individual experts until they are lost.
Validated
Reliable knowledge requires validation, ownership, and a process for resolving weak or conflicting areas.
Governed
The goal is a maintained knowledge layer with traceable structure, clear ownership, and controlled reuse.
Reusable
Once governed, operational knowledge becomes an organisational asset for teams, workflows, and AI systems.
