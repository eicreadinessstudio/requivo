How it works
Evidence becomes a governed knowledge layer that powers Requivo applications.
Requivo resolves fragmented operational evidence into validated, reusable knowledge — structured once, then used across multiple applications.
Problem
Critical operational knowledge is often scattered across systems, documents, workflows, and individual experts.
Most organisations do not fail because information does not exist. They fail because the knowledge they depend on is fragmented, difficult to validate, and often concentrated in a few individuals.
01

Expertise disappears

Critical know-how often remains embedded in individual people, workarounds, and context that never become properly structured or reusable across the organisation.

02

Knowledge never fully transfers

Teams may inherit documents, but not the reasoning, hierarchy, conflict resolution, and operational judgment behind them.

03

Answers remain fragmented

Relevant signals are distributed across documents, enterprise systems, workflows, and expert input, making them difficult to trust, maintain, and act upon together.

Solution
Requivo is designed to create a governed operational knowledge layer from fragmented enterprise evidence.

Requivo is intended for environments where critical knowledge is spread across documents, systems, workflows, and expert memory.

It is designed not only to retrieve information, but to support more structured, validated, and reusable operational clarity.

The concept should be understood as an operational knowledge system rather than a documentation platform, search tool, or generic assistant.

Why this matters
Organisations do not only need better retrieval. They need a way to turn fragmented operational evidence into governed clarity.
Fragmented
Critical knowledge often starts as disconnected evidence across documents, systems, workflows, and experts.
Weakly structured
Even when information exists, it is rarely organised in a way that reflects real operational hierarchy, currentness, and reliability.
Expert-dependent
Undocumented judgment, tacit knowledge, and edge-case reasoning stay concentrated in individuals and are hard to preserve.
Validated
Useful operational knowledge requires explicit verification and the ability to surface weak or conflicting areas.
Governed
The goal is not just a better answer, but a maintained operational knowledge layer with traceable structure and reusable logic.
Reusable
Once governed, operational knowledge can support multiple workflows across the organisation.
Use cases
Requivo is relevant wherever critical operational know-how is fragmented, weakly documented, difficult to validate, and at risk of being lost.

Operations & manufacturing

For environments where procedures, incidents, engineering context, and maintenance know-how need to remain usable beyond individual employees and disconnected systems.

Engineering & technical services

For settings where documentation, configuration, and practical judgment must remain aligned and reusable across teams and over time.

Regulated and process-heavy environments

For organisations that need stronger traceability, better validation, and more maintainable operational knowledge than conventional tools can provide.
