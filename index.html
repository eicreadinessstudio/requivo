The problem

Most organisations have documentation, but not governed operational knowledge. Critical know-how lives across systems, files, tickets, and people — and usually breaks exactly where context, judgment, and traceability matter most.

Expertise that disappears

When a long-tenured expert leaves, the organisation loses the workarounds, edge-case judgment, and operational memory built over years of practice. In most companies, that knowledge was never truly captured.

Irreplaceable · Permanent · Unrecoverable

Knowledge that never transfers

New team members inherit documents, but not the reasoning behind them. The difference between written procedure and actual operational practice remains hidden until work breaks.

Lost productivity · Slow onboarding

Answers that exist but stay fragmented

The answer may already exist — in a PDF, a Jira ticket, a config export, or a wiki page. Without governed linking and validation, it remains scattered and hard to trust.

Operational risk · Repeated search cost

Design principle

System confidence instead of abstract motion

The page now shows an interface-led product story rather than decorative graphs. That makes the product feel more credible, more operational, and more aligned with enterprise deep-tech expectations.

Visual direction

Demo-consistent, but still deep-tech

The walkthrough uses a lighter internal product canvas inside a darker shell, so the interface stays consistent with Patrick’s demo while the page around it still feels premium and technical.

Brand logic

Closer to Palantir / Mistral than generic SaaS

The structure is quieter, sharper, and more architectural: strong typography, large spacing, restrained color accents, and a product-first presentation rather than startup-style illustration.

Use cases

Requivo is relevant wherever operational expertise is critical, distributed, and difficult to preserve — especially where fragmented sources and weak documentation create risk.

Operations & Manufacturing

Preserve plant procedures, engineering know-how, and maintenance expertise before critical experience gaps become operational disruption.

Enterprise & Professional Services

Reduce time spent searching across fragmented systems and make internal expertise easier to access, validate, and reuse when it is needed.

Regulated Environments

Traceability, source linkage, and structured validation make Requivo suitable for quality, compliance, and safety-relevant workflows.
