Governed operational knowledge

Turn fragmented enterprise evidence into a governed operational knowledge layer

Requivo is a concept for transforming documents, tickets, configs, wiki content, and expert input into structured, traceable, and reusable operational knowledge — designed for environments where judgment, continuity, and verification matter.

The problem

Most organisations have documents, but not governed operational knowledge. Critical know-how is spread across files, systems, tickets, and individual experts — and usually breaks exactly where context, traceability, and judgment matter most.

Expertise that disappears

When a long-tenured expert leaves, the organisation loses the workarounds, edge-case judgment, and operational memory built over years of practice. In most companies, that knowledge was never truly captured.

Irreplaceable · Permanent · Unrecoverable

Knowledge that never transfers

New team members inherit documents, but not the reasoning behind them. The gap between written procedure and actual operational practice remains hidden until work breaks.

Slow onboarding · Repeated relearning

Answers that stay fragmented

The answer may already exist — in a PDF, a ticket, a config export, or a wiki page. Without governed linking and validation, it remains scattered, ambiguous, and hard to trust.

Operational risk · Search friction

Why this matters

Instead of adding another document repository, the goal is to create a governed operational knowledge layer that preserves context, supports validation, and reduces dependency on scattered memory.
Fragmented evidence

Procedures, tickets, configs, and wiki pages often describe the same operational reality from different angles — without a governed structure tying them together.

Validation

Operational knowledge becomes useful only when weak, incomplete, or conflicting signals can be routed into explicit expert review and resolution.

Continuity

Critical know-how should not disappear when a single expert leaves. Requivo is aimed at preserving operational continuity where memory is concentrated.

Governance

The concept is not just about retrieval. It is about traceable origin, structured linking, validation history, and controlled downstream reuse.

Use cases

Requivo is relevant wherever operational expertise is critical, distributed, and difficult to preserve — especially where fragmented sources and weak documentation create risk.

Operations & Manufacturing

Preserve plant procedures, engineering know-how, and maintenance expertise before critical experience gaps become operational disruption.

Enterprise & Professional Services

Reduce time spent searching across fragmented systems and make internal expertise easier to access, validate, and reuse when it is needed.

Regulated environments

Traceability, source linkage, and structured validation make the concept relevant for quality, compliance, and safety-relevant workflows.
