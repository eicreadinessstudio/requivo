The problem

Most organisations confuse the two. Document management tools capture what people choose to write. They cannot capture what was never written — or help organisations preserve it at scale.

Expertise that disappears

When a long-tenured expert leaves, the organisation loses the judgment, the workarounds, and the exceptions they carried for decades. No existing system captures what was never written down.

Irreplaceable · Permanent · Unrecoverable

Knowledge that never transfers

New team members inherit the manual. Not the judgment. The gap between documented procedure and how work actually gets done is never recorded — and every new hire rediscovers it from scratch.

Lost productivity · Infinite restart

Answers that exist but can't be found

In any large organisation, the answer to almost any operational question already exists — in someone's head, two floors up or two time zones away. Without a routing system, it stays there.

Operational risk · Repeated cost

The product

No hardware. No always-on recording. Browser-based. GDPR-compliant by design. Deployable without IT approval.
01 — Capture

Knowledge Capture

Requivo captures operational know-how from existing documents and expert input — without asking anyone to become an author. The system does the work of elicitation, structuring, and attribution automatically.

02 — Route

Expert Routing

When a question arises, Requivo searches the existing knowledge layer first. If the answer isn't there yet, it routes the question to exactly the right person — across any site, floor, or time zone — and captures the response permanently.
